X200 INDUSTRIAL PUMP — TECHNICAL MANUAL

1. SPECIFICATIONS
   Model: X200
   Maximum Flow Rate: 200 litres per minute
   Maximum Head: 45 metres
   Power Supply: 415V, 3-phase, 50 Hz
   Inlet/Outlet: 2 inch flanged

2. INSTALLATION
   Mount the pump on a level concrete foundation. Ensure the suction line is as short
   and straight as possible. Torque all flange bolts to 35 Nm.

3. COMMISSIONING
   Prime the pump before first start. Verify the direction of rotation matches the arrow
   on the casing. Run for 10 minutes and check for abnormal vibration or noise.

4. MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
   Daily:    Check for leaks and unusual noise.
   Monthly:  Inspect the mechanical seal and bearing temperature.
   Annually: Replace the pump seal kit and recalibrate the pressure gauge.

5. WARRANTY
   The pump is covered by a 24-month warranty from the date of commissioning. The
   warranty is void if the unit is operated beyond its rated flow or head.

6. TROUBLESHOOTING
   No flow:        Check that the pump is primed and the suction valve is open.
   Low pressure:   Inspect for a worn impeller or a blocked discharge line.
   Overheating:    Verify adequate cooling and correct bearing lubrication.
