SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 1 February 2026 between
ACME Industrial Supplies LLC ("Supplier") and VICISOFT Technologies Pvt Ltd ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Supplier shall provide maintenance and commissioning services for the X200
industrial pump units installed at the Client's Hyderabad facility.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of twelve (12) months from the
effective date and shall renew automatically unless terminated with 30 days notice.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
The Client shall pay all undisputed invoices within Net 30 days from the invoice date.
A late payment charge of 1.5% per month applies to overdue balances.

4. WARRANTY
The Supplier warrants the industrial pump for a period of 24 months from the date of
commissioning against defects in materials and workmanship.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties shall keep confidential all proprietary information disclosed under this
Agreement for a period of five (5) years following termination.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Emirate of Dubai, UAE.
